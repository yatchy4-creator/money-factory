# Product Brief — LeadRescue AI

## 1) Product Summary
LeadRescue AI helps local SMBs recover missed inbound leads (calls, web forms, DMs) by triggering instant, personalized follow-up via SMS/email and booking links. It prevents revenue leakage when staff miss calls after-hours or during peak workload.

## 2) ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

### Primary ICP
- Home services (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, cleaning)
- Med spas and dental clinics
- Legal offices and consultative agencies
- Geography: US/Canada/UK SMBs
- Team size: 3–40 employees
- Lead volume: 50–600 inbound/month

### Buyer Persona
- Owner-operator, GM, or revenue ops lead
- Pain: "We pay for leads but don’t follow up fast enough."
- Success metric: booked appointments and recovered opportunities

## 3) Core Problem
- Most SMBs lose 20–50% of potential jobs because first follow-up is late or inconsistent.
- Manual follow-up is fragile (busy staff, no standard scripts, no tracking).

## 4) Value Proposition
"Recover missed leads in under 60 seconds with AI-powered follow-up and auto-booking."

## 5) MVP Scope
- Lead intake API/webhook
- AI-generated first response + reminder sequence
- Contact attempt logs and statuses
- Simple dashboard: New / Contacted / Booked / Lost
- Retry logic + failed message queue

## 6) Pricing & Offer

### Packaging
- **Starter — $149/mo**: up to 300 leads/mo, 1 location
- **Growth — $349/mo**: up to 1,000 leads/mo, multi-channel follow-up
- **Pro — $699/mo**: 3,000 leads/mo, multi-location, priority support

### Usage Add-ons
- $0.15 per extra lead processed
- Optional done-for-you setup: $499 one-time

### Offer
- 14-day trial
- "10 booked opportunities in 30 days or we work free until you do" (service credit terms)

## 7) Differentiators
- SMB-focused onboarding in <48 hours
- Built-in anti-failure: retries, dead-letter queue, alerting
- Script personalization by vertical and lead source

## 8) Key Metrics
- Lead response time (target: <60s)
- Contact rate
- Appointment-booked rate
- Recovered revenue estimate
- Churn and expansion rate
