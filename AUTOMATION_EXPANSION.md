# Automation Expansion (Additional Money Methods)

## 1) Missed-Call Text-Back Service (Standalone)
- **Monetization:** $99–$299/mo per location
- **Semi-automation:** trigger SMS fallback on missed call webhook
- **Safety:** human escalation keywords ("urgent", "legal", "medical") route to staff

## 2) Review-Request Automation
- **Monetization:** add-on $79/mo
- **Semi-automation:** post-service trigger sends review ask with smart timing
- **Safety:** suppress messages for unresolved/negative tickets

## 3) Re-activation Campaigns (Old Leads)
- **Monetization:** setup fee + performance bonus
- **Semi-automation:** AI segments dormant leads and drafts personalized win-back
- **Safety:** cap daily sends, enforce opt-out handling, compliance checks

## 4) No-Show Recovery Assistant
- **Monetization:** $149/mo add-on
- **Semi-automation:** auto-reminder + rebook links when appointment missed
- **Safety:** frequency caps and stop conditions after response

## 5) Quote Follow-Up Copilot
- **Monetization:** % of recovered deals or tiered SaaS
- **Semi-automation:** follow up unresolved estimates at 24h/72h/7d
- **Safety:** require approval for high-value custom quotes

## 6) Upsell/Cross-Sell Nurture
- **Monetization:** premium tier differentiator
- **Semi-automation:** trigger campaigns by completed service type
- **Safety:** strict consent segmentation and per-customer throttle limits

## Safe Automation Principles
- Always include clear opt-out language
- Keep human override paths visible
- Log every outbound action with reason
- Add retry limits and dead-letter queue for failures
- Avoid autonomous commitments (pricing/legal promises) without review
