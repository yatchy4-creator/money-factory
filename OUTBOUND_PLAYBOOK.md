# Outbound Playbook

## 1) Cold DM Script (Short)
"Hey {{first_name}} — noticed {{business_name}} is running lead-gen, but many SMBs lose bookings from missed calls/forms. We help recover those leads automatically in <60 seconds with AI follow-up. Worth a quick 10-min teardown of your current flow?"

## 2) Cold Email Script (Pain + ROI)
Subject: quick idea to recover missed leads at {{business_name}}

Hi {{first_name}},

Most SMBs lose 20–50% of paid leads from delayed follow-up.

We built LeadRescue AI to automatically follow up with missed calls/forms in under 60 seconds and drive more booked jobs.

If useful, I can share a 3-minute loom with a custom revenue-recovery estimate for {{business_name}}.

Open to that?

— {{sender}}

## 3) Follow-up Sequence
- Day 2: send mini case study
- Day 4: send estimated recovered revenue math
- Day 7: "close file" message
